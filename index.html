About Me

Tamar is currently a political writer and editor making the transition into a career in web development.

In the past, Tamar has worked at the United Nations as a correspondent focused on climate change and served as the director of an award-winning food pantry. In both of those roles, Tamar learned that hard work -- and attention to detail -- are vital to getting the job done.

Now, she is focused on gaining skills to thrive as a full-time developer.

A current Bertelsmann Data Science scholar and student of Columbia Engineering's Coding Bootcamp, Tamar is set on launching new career path by the Summer of 2019.
